ABOUT

My passion lies in exploring the intersection of software, systems, and creative problem-solving. I enjoy building tools and interfaces that are not only functional but thoughtfully designed, focusing on clarity, efficiency, and meaningful user experiences. Whether experimenting with web technologies or scripting automation tools, I’m driven by curiosity and the satisfaction of turning ideas into working systems.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri. My interests span Python development, modern web technologies, shell scripting, and open-source collaboration. Through projects and continuous experimentation, I aim to deepen my understanding of how software systems work and contribute to communities that build technology openly and collaboratively.
